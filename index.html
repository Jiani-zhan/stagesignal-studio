Personal Marketing Science App

From artistic intuition to marketing science.

This project studies Hamilton Immersive: a proposal to adapt the Broadway musical Hamilton into an interactive, walk-through production led by Punchdrunk. StageSignal Studio converts benchmark, audience, and survey data into segmentation insights, demand projections, pricing recommendations, and a decision-ready launch memo.

The analysis treats Hamilton as a top-tier IP with strong historical performance, broad digital influence, and a clear fit for immersive theatre experimentation.

Research Background: Hamilton Immersive

Project context and market assumptions

  • Study object: adapting Broadway's Hamilton into an immersive theatre format.
  • Production context: proposed as a new venture direction for Punchdrunk.
  • Historical signal: about $1.109B cumulative Broadway gross (Jul 2015-Oct 2025).
  • Demand signal: average sold-seat rate around 99.69% over the same period.
  • Research goal: test audience fit, demand risk, pricing strategy, and launch viability.
